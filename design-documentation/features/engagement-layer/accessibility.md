# Engagement Layer – Accessibility

## Motion

- Provide reduced-motion alternatives for confetti and animated badges.

## Screen Readers

- Announce achievements succinctly without overwhelming the user.

## Visual Design

- Ensure icons and badges meet contrast guidelines and are not solely color-coded.
